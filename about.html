About Us

Providing quality service since the past five decades, Raju driving school has earned both credibility and people’s trust. We will find you the best driving instructor to help you pass the driving test with ease and confidence. We at Raju driving school believe that just learning how to drive is not enough and that as responsible citizens it is important to have a basic sense of road which we will instill in you. Hence, post the training session you will emerge as a confident, responsible driver. Besides, driving has almost become a necessary survival skill in this day and age. So, come join Raju Driving School to discover the confident, independent and responsible driver in you.

With years of experience, we have built a proper team of well experienced instructors dedicated to train students in two wheelers and heavy vehicles. Also, we have lady trainers for lady learners. Taking into account the increasing number of road accidents in the state, we have included additional theory classes which will help to develop a better idea of the vehicle and the road. We have flexible timings to suit your busy schedule. Also, we provide personalized training specific to your vehicle as well as practice sessions for license holders on request.

Our Mission

Our mission at Raju Driving School is to provide each student with proper knowledge and skill set to help them become safe, responsible, and confident drivers. Hence, we strive to promote attitudes associated with a safe and successful life-long driving experience.
